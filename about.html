About

a quiet place for stories to rest

This garden grows slowly.

Each story is a seed — planted with care, watered with patience, and left to grow in its own time.

They are for little ones learning to listen, and for grown-ups who still remember how.

There is no rush here. No lessons to be learned, only quiet moments to be shared.

So come in, sit down, and stay as long as you like.

The stories will be here when you return.

— from the garden, with love
