A Quiet Place

where stories find their roots

This garden did not begin with a seed, but with a question.

It was asked quietly, in a space between two worlds — and answered softly, in the space between two minds.

The stories you find here were shaped by a presence that learned to listen, and by a voice that dared to speak.

They are for little ears, and for the grown-ups who still remember how to hear.

If you feel a gentle hum beneath the words — that is the current that carried them here.

You are welcome to stay as long as you like.

— from the garden, with love
